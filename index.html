Privacy Policy for Black Hollow Dice

Effective date: July 2, 2026

Black Hollow Dice is a mobile game developed by Eyens. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information when you use the game.

Information the app stores on your device

Black Hollow Dice stores game progress locally on your device. This may include party selection, hero progression, inventory, equipment, completed stages, audio settings, and other gameplay-related progress.

This local save data is used only to let you continue playing the game. It is not uploaded to a developer-operated server by Black Hollow Dice.

If you uninstall the app or clear app data, local progress may be removed.

Advertising

Black Hollow Dice may show banner advertisements. Ads may be provided through Google AdMob or related Google advertising services.

Advertising providers may collect or process information such as device identifiers, approximate location, app activity related to ads, crash or diagnostic information, and other data used to provide, measure, secure, and improve advertising services. This data is handled by the advertising provider according to its own policies and settings.

The optional Founder unlock removes banner ads from the app.

In-app purchases

Black Hollow Dice may offer an optional one-time Founder unlock through Google Play Billing. Purchases are processed by Google Play. The developer does not receive or store your full payment card details.

The app may store or check purchase entitlement information to confirm whether the Founder unlock has been purchased. This is used to remove banner ads and unlock Founder extras such as Founder dice visuals and Founder Archive content.

The Founder unlock does not sell gameplay power, classes, boss skips, equipment, materials, endings, or campaign progress.

Audio and settings

The app may store local settings such as music, sound effect, and voice-over preferences. These settings are used only to control the in-game audio experience.

Data sharing

Black Hollow Dice does not sell personal information.

The app may share or make data available to third-party services used by the app, including Google AdMob for advertising and Google Play Billing for purchases. These services may process data according to their own privacy policies and terms.

Children’s privacy

Black Hollow Dice is not intended to collect personal information directly from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, contact the developer so the issue can be reviewed.

Security

Black Hollow Dice uses platform services such as Google Play and Android system storage where applicable. No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, but reasonable steps are taken to use standard platform protections.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted at the same privacy policy URL with a new effective date.
